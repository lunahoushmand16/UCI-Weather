How to Use the WeatherApp:

WeatherApp is a simple tool to check the weather quickly. Sign in to personalize your experience—your data is stored in your browser's local storage. Just enter a city name or ZIP code, hit search, and instantly view today's temperature, conditions, and tomorrow's forecast. A map will also show the searched location for better visualization. Enjoy exploring the weather with ease!
